Bread and Butter Pizza

Best No-Dough Pizza Hack Ever!

Ingredients

  • Thick slices of several day-old bread
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Place six thick slices of bread on the baking pan, separating each piece a few inches apart
  2. Generously butter both sides of each slice
  3. Grate a block of fresh Parmesan cheese over the topâ€”not enough to pile up, but enough to cover the bread from crust to crust
  4. gently press the cheese down to secure it, then flip the bread cheese side down. The cheese and butter will meld together and caramelize onto the bottom of the pan
  5. Add the pizza sauce on to the opposite buttered side, smoothing it from edge to edge.
  6. load up each slice with mozarella cheese and pepperoni
  7. Bake until browned and melty
